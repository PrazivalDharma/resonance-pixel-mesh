Once you're in a "Rabbit Hole," use the **3D Range Slider**.

## 3. Your Intellect Profile
As you explore, the **Agape Engine** strings your interests together. This builds your **Intellect Profile**—a map of your unique genius. Use this to "Plug-and-Play" into the global workforce where your strengths are needed most.
